Brisket

Description

Make this brisket for someone and I guarantee the brisket will not be the only thing gushing juices. You are welcome.

Ingredients

  • Big ole packer Brisket
  • 100 g salt
  • 100 g black pepper

Steps

  1. Trim brisket ever so gently.
  2. Coat brisket in salt and pepper.
  3. Smoke around 14 hours at 225 degrees F.
  4. Wrap and let rest for 4 hours.
  5. Let the juices flow forth.
