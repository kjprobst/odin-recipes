Pizza

Description

This is old school italian grandma pizza. Not actually Italian, but New Jersey Guido at least.

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 60 g water
  • 15 g dry yeast
  • 48 g olive oil
  • 12 g salt

Steps

  1. Heat water to 95 degrees F.
  2. Mix yeast and water and let sit for 15 minutes.
  3. Mix in rest of the ingredients and knead for 5 minutes.
  4. Cover dough and let rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Cut dough in half and roll out into 12 inch circles.
